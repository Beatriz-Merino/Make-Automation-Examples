# Simple Lead Notification Workflow

## Goal
Receive a quick notification when a new lead arrives through a website form.

## Workflow example
Website form → webhook → Make → email notification

## Main steps
1. A user submits a contact or lead form
2. The form sends the data to a webhook
3. Make receives the submission
4. The information is formatted into a simple message
5. An email notification is sent for follow-up

## Use case
Useful for small business websites, landing pages and service offers that need fast lead visibility without a complex system.
