# Make Automation Examples

Examples of lightweight automations and practical workflow systems for digital operations, lead capture and business processes.

## About
This repository documents simple and practical automation workflows designed to improve organization, lead handling and digital operations.

## Includes
- lead capture workflow
- email notification flow
- lead sheet workflow
- booking request workflow
- form to sheet and email workflow
- client intake workflow
- simple lead notification workflow

## Focus
The goal is to show lightweight automation ideas that are realistic, useful and easy to adapt for service websites and small business workflows.

## Use cases
- contact form handling
- lead capture and follow-up
- booking request collection
- intake form processing
- spreadsheet-based lead organization
- email notifications for business events
