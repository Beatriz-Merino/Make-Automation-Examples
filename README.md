# Make Automation Examples

Examples of lightweight automations and practical workflow systems built for digital operations, lead capture and business processes.

## Includes
- Lead capture workflows
- Form to webhook automations
- Email and notification flows
- Simple business process automations
- Practical Make scenarios for digital operations

## Use cases
- Website forms connected to workflows
- Lead collection and organization
- Internal alerts and notifications
- Lightweight admin systems
- Automation support for small businesses and digital services

## Focus
This repository documents practical automation ideas and workflow examples designed to improve efficiency, organization and digital operations without unnecessary complexity.
