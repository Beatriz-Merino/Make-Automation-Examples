# Client Intake Workflow

## Goal
Collect initial client information through a form and organize it automatically for easier follow-up.

## Workflow example
Intake form → webhook → Make → Google Sheets or email notification

## Main steps
1. A user completes an intake form
2. The data is sent to a webhook
3. Make receives and processes the submission
4. The information is stored in a structured format
5. A follow-up action or notification is triggered

## Use case
Useful for service businesses, consultations, onboarding flows and small business client management.
