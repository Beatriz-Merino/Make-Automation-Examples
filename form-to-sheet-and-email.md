# Form to Sheet and Email

## Goal
Send form data into a spreadsheet and trigger an email notification at the same time.

## Workflow example
Website form → webhook → Make → Google Sheets + email notification

## Main steps
1. A user submits a website form
2. The data is sent to a webhook
3. Make receives and processes the submission
4. A new row is added to Google Sheets
5. An email notification is sent

## Use case
Useful for contact forms, lead capture systems, service inquiries and simple client intake workflows.
