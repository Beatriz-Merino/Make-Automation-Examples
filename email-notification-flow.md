# Email Notification Flow

## Goal
Send a simple notification email when a lead, form submission or business event is received.

## Workflow example
Form submission → webhook → Make → email notification

## Main steps
1. A user submits a form
2. The data is sent to a webhook
3. Make receives the payload
4. The workflow formats the information
5. A notification email is sent

## Use case
Useful for lead capture, contact forms, booking requests and simple internal alerts.
